Principal Investigator

Jonathan Lovell, PI

jflovell@buffalo.edu

Jonathan Lovell is a Distinguished Professor in the Biomedical Engineering Department at UB. He received his PhD from the Institute for Biomaterials and Biomedical Engineering at the University of Toronto in 2012 in the lab of Gang Zheng. He previously completed his MSc at McMaster University and his undergraduate degree at the University of Waterloo.
